Bonagrew is inspired by the good-humoured and hard-working Irish gentleman. A man who mingles with ease between work and play; a man of great company.

Named after the coastal townland in Co. Wicklow, Bonagrew makes impeccably crafted neckties, bowties and scarves with a relaxed attitude.
